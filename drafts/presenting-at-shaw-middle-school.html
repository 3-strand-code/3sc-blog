Presenting at Shaw Middle School

Published: Fri 01 January 2016
By Eric Carmichael

In community.

tags: presenting community coding

We get the opportunity to talk to students in our community every so often, this time we're talking to a group of students in the On Track Academy at Shaw Middle School.

They were a great group of enthusiastic students... in fact, one bright student is already doing animation and experimenting with making her own webpages! They asked lots of questions, got excited, and applied for the program. Can't wait to see where we are a year from now!

Thanks to Tualau Fale for inviting us! He's a great guy and we hope to hang out again soon!
